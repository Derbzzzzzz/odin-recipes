Pancakes

Description

It is truly difficult to find someone who dislikes pancakes. They are a staple of the American breakfast.

All you will need for this recipe is a large bowl for mixing ingredients and a griddle or frying pan. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt (optional: more to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons butter (melted)

Steps

  1. Sift together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar in large bowl. Create a well in the center and fill it with the milk, egg, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
