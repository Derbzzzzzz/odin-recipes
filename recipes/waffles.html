Waffles

Description

It is truly difficult to find someone who dislikes waffles. They are a staple of the American breakfast.

You can keep the batter covered in the fridge for up to a week. Just be sure to whisk well before using again. You may also use whole wheat flour for this recipe. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron. Beat eggs in large bowl with hand beater until fluffy. Beat in flour, milk, vegetable oil, sugar, baking powder, salt and vanilla, just until smooth.
  2. Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron. Cook until golden brown. Serve hot.
