Scrambled Eggs

Description

It is truly difficult to find someone who dislikes scrambled eggs. They are a staple of the American breakfast.

The right way to scramble eggs. There is more to just mixing eggs and cooking! This will make a believer out of you. Let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon mayo or salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon margarine or butter
  • 1 of pinch salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon water (Optional)

Steps

  1. In a cup or small bowl, whisk together the eggs, mayonnaise and water using a fork. Melt margarine in a skillet over low heat.
  2. Pour in the eggs, and stir constantly as they cook. Remove the eggs to a plate when they are set, but still moist. Do not over cook. Never add salt or pepper until eggs are on plate, but these are also good without.
